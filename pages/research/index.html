OUR MISSION

Research Interest & Vision

It is amazing to see the amount of solar energy Earth receives every second. At the same time, it is critical to address how we convert such abundant energy sources into chemical or electrical energy. Bulk of our research revolves around designing semiconductors to harness light, tailor properties, and control excited-state dynamics for solar cells, photocatalysis, sensors, and optoelectronics.

Synthesis-Structure Correlation

Elucidating material parameters across chemical vapor deposition, sputtering, and spray pyrolysis.

Time-Resolved Spectroscopy

Probing exciton and charge carrier dynamics across femtosecond to microsecond timescales.

Solar Fuels & H₂ Evolution

Cost-effective photocatalysts for solar hydrogen production at scale and CO₂ valorisation.
