## Research Focus: Advanced Materials for Energy Storage and Sensors

DFCL focuses on:
- **Solid-State Energy Storage:** MXenes, MOF derivatives, supercapacitors, and battery electrodes
- **Smart Sensors:** Chemiresistive & optical gas sensors for sub-ppm H₂ leak detection and wearable biosensors
- **Microfabrication:** Thin-film sputtering, CVD, spin-coating, and in situ impedance characterization
